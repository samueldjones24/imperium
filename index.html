Welcome to Imperium

Hi there! My name is Josh Jones and welcome to my gym, Imperium. After 5 years at the Royal Marines and competing at CrossFit tournaments, I wanted to open my own gym space that focuses on physical and mental strength. I'd love you to join us for a class or even a session on the weights. Either way, I'd be happy to answer any questions, share some tips or even do a bit of spotting!

Open-plan gym with high quality equipment

With a climbing section at the back, workout space with general cardio equipment (e.g. bikes, SkiErgs) at the front and a separate weights/strongman room, the gym offers plenty of variety so you can work on any part of your training program with us. Other facilities include male/female changing rooms with showers and on-site toilets.

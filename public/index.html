Welcome to Happy Walks

Hi there! My name is Isaac and welcome to my local dog walking club, Happy Walks. We are a friendly (and furry) bunch based in Romiley, Stockport and I would love to see your dog join one of our walks - I'm sure they'll love it and go home a happy pup.

What to expect on the walk

Our walks take place either individually or in small groups of no more than three dogs to ensure your dog gets the attention it deserves. While we walk your dog, we will stay in the local vicinity to avoid lengthy unsettling car journeys. Right now, we only cover the areas of Romiley and Bredbury, but if you fall just outside our range, please get in touch and we can sort something out.
